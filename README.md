# 🚀 Welcome to yallaManwha v1.0.0!

We are incredibly excited to bring you the first official public release of **yallaManwha**! 📖✨ 

This app is designed to provide you with the fastest, most beautiful, and smoothest reading experience for all your favorite manga, manhua, and manhwa.

---

## 🌟 What's New?

### 📖 Perfect Reading Experience
* **Lightning-Fast Loading**: Enjoy seamless reading with super-fast image loading that caches images so you never have to wait for pages to load.
* **Smart Chapter Tracker**: Easily keep track of which chapters you have read and which ones are next.
* **Quick Swipe Gestures**: Read a chapter by accident? Simply swipe a chapter item to instantly mark it as unread again.

### 🌍 Read in Your Language
* **Full Translation Support**: Easily switch the application interface into your preferred language.
* **Remembers Your Preference**: Choose your language once and the app will remember it every time you open it.

### 🌓 Comfortable Day & Night Reading
* **Beautiful Dark & Light Modes**: Enjoy bright, crisp designs in Light Mode, or switch to eye-friendly Dark Mode for reading late at night.
* **Saved Theme Preferences**: The app remembers your preferred theme so you never have to adjust it twice.

---

## ⚡ Improvements & Stability

* **Instant Improvements**: We have enabled instant update capabilities! You will receive hotfixes and minor updates instantly in the background without needing to manually update the app from the app store.
* **Clean Launch Screens**: A refreshed visual layout and gorgeous new splash screens for both iOS and Android.
* **Under-the-Hood Polish**: We have tuned performance, minimized memory usage, and improved crash protection to keep your reading sessions running smoothly.
